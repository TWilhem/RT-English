Location
Networks and Telecommunications, also known as R&T, is a program offered in more than twenty institutions, each providing specific academic tracks.
This presentation focuses on the R&T program at the IUT of Béziers, including its specializations, job opportunities, and career prospects.
Subject
In R&T, you will learn a lot of things
such as mathematics, how to connect to machines, how information is transmitted via fiber optics or electricity, how to deploy applications, how to build and optimize networks, and how to automate tasks using programming languages like Python, JavaScript, Bash, Arduino,
and a lot more.
Field of study
In your second year, you will have to choose a specialization. At IUT Béziers, there are two options:

- The first is "Cybersecurity," where you will learn
how to attack, monitor, analyze, and protect infrastructure.

- The second is "Cloud," where you will learn
how to deploy, use, share, manage, and secure infrastructure.
Environment
IUT Béziers is not limited to the classroom.
You have access to a wide range of
activities, equipment, and knowledge at your disposal at any time.
