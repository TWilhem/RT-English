Multisport Tournament

On April 10, 2025, the first Student Multisport Tournament took place. The program included padel, volleyball, pétanque, video games... And above all, more than 100 students gathered.

I Film My Training

The Béziers IUT won the 2025 edition of the national contest "I Film My Training". The TC department received the Diamond Prize and the MMI department two Bronze Prizes.

The Chrysalis of Red Satin

On April 29, 2025, at the Franciscans Theater in Béziers, the TC department presented the short film made by 2nd and 3rd year TC students after a two-year project.

AI DAY

On April 8, 2025, the Béziers IUT hosted a day themed "Artificial Intelligence for Industry: Challenges and Perspectives". The event aimed to demystify AI.

Alternance Forum

On April 3, the Béziers IUT organized an alternance forum for all departments to connect recruiting companies with students seeking contracts for September.

Conference: Witches and Wizards

On March 19, 2025, the IUT hosted a conference by Michelle Zancarini-Fournel presenting her essay "Letter to Young Feminists," exploring the history of witches and their links to current feminist struggles.

6th Hackathon

From March 11 to 14, 2025, the IUT organized the 6th Hackathon, focused on "Ecological Transition and Sustainable Development."

Open Doors

On Saturday, February 8, 2025, the IUT held its open house to introduce the IUT to high school students and allow them to meet with students and teachers.

Continuing Studies

On January 23, the IUT organized a forum to present study opportunities after completing a Bac+3 degree.
